  • Do we need to attend every day?

    You just need to be present every Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. However, on Saturdays and Sundays, we start at 9:00 AM sometimes.

  • Is this internship paid or unpaid?

    Unfortunately, at the moment, the internship program at Dialogika remains unpaid, as we are still a new and developing company and lack the financial stability to provide monetary compensation. We can only hope for better prospects in the future for our interns.

  • Is there any compensation provided by the company, such as transportation and lunch?

    Unfortunately, we are unable to provide perks such as lunch and transportation at this time. Occasionally, Dialogika organizes group meals during certain events, but these are still quite rare. If you find this unsatisfactory, we kindly ask that you refrain from applying to this program.

  • What if I don't complete the internship program?

    If you are unable to complete this internship program, it is regrettable that you will not receive an eCertificate from us, and it is also unfortunate that if any companies ask about you in the future, we will not be able to speak much about you. Make sure your heart is ready if you want to join.

  • What happens if my class schedule and internship hours overlap?

    During the interview, we will inquire about your class schedule. Typically, if you are unavailable on Tuesdays, Thursdays, Saturdays, and Sundays, we would advise against taking this internship program. However, if there are only two days that you cannot attend, we can still consider your participation.

  • How can I apply for this internship?

    To initiate the application process, please follow these steps: begin by selecting the desired position and internship program. Next, access the registration form and provide your personal details.
